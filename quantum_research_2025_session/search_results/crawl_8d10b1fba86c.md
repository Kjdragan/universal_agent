### GCV Leadership Society
The Leadership Society is comprised of CVCs from across the globe who recognise the individual and collective benefits of coming together to form a community that advances the industry in impactful ways. Members benefit from the full range of GCV services that enable them to successfully navigate today’s innovation and investment landscape.
 
### GCV Institute
Dedicated to corporate venturing industry advancement, staying power and impact, the Institute unleashes the collective power of corporations as strategic investors and innovation partners. It supports the industry with professional development and certification informed by GCV Keystone’s unique operational benchmarking. By CVCs for CVCs, the Institute convenes powerful communities of practice. 
 
### GCV News and Analysis
We cover the companies, deals, people, and emerging investment trends shaping the future of innovation and capital. Through our website, newsletter, social channels, and reports, we deliver breaking news, informed analysis and unparalleled access to industry leaders. 
### About us
GCV provides the global corporate venturing community and their ecosystem partners with the information, insights and access needed to drive impactful open innovation. Across our three services - News & Analysis, Community & Events, and the GCV Institute - we create a network-rich environment for global innovation and capital to meet and thrive. At the heart of our community sits the GCV Leadership Society, providing privileged access to all our services and resources.
