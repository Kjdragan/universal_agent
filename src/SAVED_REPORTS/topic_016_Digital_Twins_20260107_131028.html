Digital twin technology adoption accelerated in manufacturing, supply chain, and healthcare by December 2025. Real-time synchronization and predictive capabilities drove enterprise value.

Future Outlook (2026+)

Digital twins will achieve broader adoption through 2026 with AI integration, improved simulation capabilities, and expanded industry use cases driving growth.
