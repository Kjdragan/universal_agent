Semiconductor Manufacturing and Chiplets

Emerging Technologies 2025 | Report #005 | January 7, 2026

Executive Summary

Chiplet architectures became central to semiconductor manufacturing in 2025, driving performance gains and cost efficiency. Advanced packaging at 2nm/3nm nodes and AI chip specialization characterized the industry transformation.

Key Breakthroughs in 2025

  • Chiplet-based processor designs mainstream
  • Advanced packaging at 2nm/3nm
  • AI chip specialization
  • US/EU chip fabrication investments

Market Investment & Growth

$50B+ in new fab construction; CHIPS Act impact

Industry Applications

  • Data center AI accelerators
  • High-performance computing
  • Automotive chips
  • Mobile processors

Future Outlook (2026+)

Semiconductor manufacturing will continue evolving through 2026 with advanced packaging, chiplet architectures, and specialized AI accelerators driving industry transformation.
