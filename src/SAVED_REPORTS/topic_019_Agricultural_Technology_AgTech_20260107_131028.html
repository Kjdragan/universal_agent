Agricultural Technology (AgTech)

Emerging Technologies 2025 | Report #019 | January 7, 2026

Executive Summary

Precision farming in 2025 dominated by AI-powered autonomous machinery and drone-satellite imaging. Vertical farming optimization and gene-edited crops drove agricultural innovation.

Key Breakthroughs in 2025

  • AI autonomous farm equipment
  • Drone + satellite imaging integration
  • Vertical farming optimization
  • Gene-edited crops

Market Investment & Growth

$50B AgTech investment

Industry Applications

  • Precision planting and fertilization
  • Crop health monitoring
  • Autonomous harvesting
  • Sustainable farming

Future Outlook (2026+)

AgTech will continue transforming agriculture through 2026 with AI automation, precision farming, and sustainable practices driving productivity and sustainability.
