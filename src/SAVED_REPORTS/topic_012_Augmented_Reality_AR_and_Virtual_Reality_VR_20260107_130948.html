Augmented Reality (AR) and Virtual Reality (VR)

Emerging Technologies 2025 | Report #012 | January 7, 2026

Executive Summary

AR/VR headsets moved to ultra-light designs with micro-OLED displays exceeding 4K resolution in 2025. Pass-through AR advancement and enterprise adoption acceleration characterized market development.

Key Breakthroughs in 2025

  • Ultra-light pancake lens designs
  • Micro-OLED >4K displays
  • Pass-through AR advancement
  • Enterprise adoption acceleration

Market Investment & Growth

$50B XR market; Apple Vision Pro impact

Industry Applications

  • Enterprise training and simulation
  • Remote collaboration
  • Gaming and entertainment
  • Retail visualization

Future Outlook (2026+)

AR/VR will achieve broader adoption through 2026 with improved hardware, reduced form factors, and expanded enterprise use cases driving market growth.
