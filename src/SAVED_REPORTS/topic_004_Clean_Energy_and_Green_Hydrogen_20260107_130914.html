Clean Energy and Green Hydrogen

Emerging Technologies 2025 | Report #004 | January 7, 2026

Executive Summary

Green hydrogen projects expanded to 200+ low-emission facilities globally by September 2025, with production costs reaching $2/kg in key markets. Policy support from EU, US, and Asian governments accelerated deployment, while electrolyzer efficiency improvements and renewable energy cost reductions drove economic viability.

Key Breakthroughs in 2025

  • 200+ green hydrogen projects operational
  • Cost reduction to $2/kg in key markets
  • Electrolyzer efficiency improvements
  • Policy support in EU, US, Asia

Market Investment & Growth

$500B+ global green hydrogen investment announced

Industry Applications

  • Industrial decarbonization
  • Energy storage
  • Transportation fuel
  • Power generation

Future Outlook (2026+)

Green hydrogen is poised for continued growth through 2026 and beyond, with accelerating adoption, improving economics, and expanding use cases driving market expansion.
