Russia-Ukraine War Developments: January 2026

Report Date: January 14, 2026

Period Covered: January 1-14, 2026

Conflict Duration: Nearly 4 years (since February 2022 full-scale invasion)

Report Type: Comprehensive Military and Strategic Analysis

Executive Summary

January 2026 has witnessed a significant escalation in the Russia-Ukraine conflict, marked by Russia's deployment of advanced missile systems, continued offensives along multiple fronts, and growing diplomatic tensions. The period is characterized by Russia's first combat use of the nuclear-capable Oreshnik ballistic missile, intensifying drone warfare, and territorial gains in eastern Ukraine. Meanwhile, diplomatic efforts led by the Trump administration face significant obstacles as Moscow maintains its maximalist demands and Kyiv continues its long-range strike campaign against Russian infrastructure.
