Executive Summary

Starlink added 4.6M new customers in 2025, reaching record growth and improved speeds with 20ms average latency. Competitor launches from Amazon Kuiper and OneTier accelerated market development.

Key Breakthroughs in 2025

  • Starlink 4.6M new subscribers
  • Reduced satellite de-orbit rates by 70%
  • Lower latency (20ms average)
  • Competitor launches (Amazon Kuiper, OneTier)

Market Investment & Growth

SpaceX valuation $200B; global space economy $500B

Industry Applications

  • Global broadband access
  • Maritime and aviation connectivity
  • Remote sensing
  • Defense communications

Future Outlook (2026+)

Satellite internet will continue expanding global connectivity through 2026 with improved speeds, lower latency, and expanded coverage driving market adoption.
