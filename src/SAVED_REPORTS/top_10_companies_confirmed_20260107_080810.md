## Research Approach

For each of these 10 companies, I will conduct deep-dive research including:

### 1. Business Overview
- Company history and evolution
- Business model and revenue segments
- Geographic presence
- Product/service portfolio
- Leadership team
- Mission and vision

### 2. Financial Analysis (5-Year: 2020-2024)
- Income statement data (Revenue, Net Income, EPS)
- Balance sheet data (Assets, Debt, Equity)
- Cash flow data (Operating FCF, FCF)
- Market data (Market cap, stock price metrics)
- Key ratios (Margins, ROE, ROA, Debt/Equity)
- Growth metrics (CAGR, YoY growth)

### 3. Competitive Positioning
- Market share by segment
- Major competitors
- Competitive advantages/moat
- Industry ranking
- Barriers to entry

### 4. Growth Drivers
- Product innovation pipeline
- Market expansion opportunities
- M&A strategy
- Digital transformation
- Secular tailwinds
- Capex and R&D investments

### 5. Risk Factors
- Regulatory/legal risks
- Competitive threats
- Technology disruption
- Financial risks
- Operational risks
- ESG/Climate risks

### 6. Investment Thesis
- Bull case (catalysts, upside potential)
- Bear case (risks, downside)
- Fair value estimate
- Investment rating (Buy/Hold/Sell)
