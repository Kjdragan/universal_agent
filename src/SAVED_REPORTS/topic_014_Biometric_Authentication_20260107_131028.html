Biometric Authentication

Emerging Technologies 2025 | Report #014 | January 7, 2026

Executive Summary

Multimodal contactless biometrics dominated authentication security technology in December 2025. Contactless authentication, behavioral biometrics, and privacy-preserving authentication characterized innovation.

Key Breakthroughs in 2025

  • Multimodal biometric systems
  • Contactless authentication
  • Behavioral biometrics
  • Privacy-preserving authentication

Market Investment & Growth

$40B biometrics market

Industry Applications

  • Mobile device security
  • Physical access control
  • Payment authentication
  • Identity verification

Future Outlook (2026+)

Biometric authentication will continue evolving through 2026 with multimodal systems, improved privacy protections, and expanded use cases driving adoption.
