Three things that correct your mental model

  • It's Anthropic, not GLM. Proactive builds run --cody-mode hybrid → the code-writing /goal session is Anthropic Max Opus (same as /demo). GLM is only the demo's own runtime inference. So proactive builds spend Anthropic Max, not ZAI — and the failure has nothing to do with GLM translation.
