- **US Delegation:** Special Envoys Steve Witkoff and Jared Kushner, Army Secretary Dan Driscoll, Gen. Alexus Grynkewich

### 2. INFRASTRUCTURE ATTACKS & HUMANITARIAN CRISIS

**Scale of Devastation**
- **1.2 million people** affected by power and heat outages
- **800,000 in Kyiv** alone without electricity/heating
- **Temperatures:** Minus 18°C (0°F) - coldest winter of the war
- **Outages lasting up to 19 hours**

**Strategic Shift in Russian Tactics**
- Moved beyond electrical grid strikes to targeting **centralized thermal plants (TETs)**
- Exploits Soviet-era urban architecture vulnerability
- British intelligence: **55,000 drones** used in 2025 (5x increase from 2024)
- **8.5 gigawatts** of generation capacity destroyed (nearly half of typical consumption)
- **375 drones + 21 missiles** in single overnight bombardment during peace talks

**Humanitarian Impact**
- Public health crisis: spike in respiratory illnesses, pneumonia in children
- Apartment temperatures falling to 9°C during outages
- Residents sleeping in coats, heating bricks on stoves
- "Invincibility points" established for warmth and charging
- Ukraine lost ~50% of domestic gas production

**Funding Gap:** $1 billion urgently needed for emergency energy equipment

**Sources:** BBC, CNN, Al Jazeera, UN News, Reuters, Atlantic Council, UkraineWorld, The Guardian, New York Times, Euronews, Kyiv Independent
