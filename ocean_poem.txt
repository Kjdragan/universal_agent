The ocean breathes with tide and time,
A rhythmic, deep, and ancient rhyme.
Beneath the waves where shadows sleep,
The secrets of the world run deep.

A surface glass of shimmering light,
That turns to fury in the night.
With crashing waves against the shore,
It whispers peace, then roars for more.

A boundless blue, a vast unknown,
Where coral cities are overthrown.
A world untamed, forever free,
The endless, wild, and restless sea.
