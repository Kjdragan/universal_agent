# Briefing Template

Use this exact structure for every meeting briefing. Keep each section to 2-4 lines.
The entire briefing should fit on one printed page.

```markdown
# Meeting Briefing: [Event Title]
**Date:** [date and time]
**Duration:** [duration]
**Attendees:** [comma-separated list]

## Attendee Profiles
[For each attendee: Name, Title, Company. One sentence background.
 Focus on: decision-making authority, product area, recent public activity.]

## Company Context
[2-3 sentences maximum. Recent funding, product launches, strategic pivots.
 Prioritize information relevant to the meeting's likely topic.]

## Suggested Talking Points
[3-5 bullet points. Each should be specific enough that the reader could lead
 a 2-minute conversation on it. Reference the attendee's role or recent activity.]

## Recent News
[2-3 bullets with source attribution. Focus on news the attendee would find
 relevant to their role, not generic company headlines.]

## Openers
[1-2 conversation starters grounded in the attendee's recent public activity
 (conference talks, blog posts, product announcements, social media).]
```
