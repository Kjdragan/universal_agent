Fire sleeps beneath the stone,
In chambers deep, unseen, unknown.
A molten heart begins to beat,
Awakening with rising heat.

The mountain trembles, cracks, and sighs,
As ash ascends into the skies.
A glowing river, fierce and bright,
Paints the darkness of the night.

Creation's forge, destruction's hand,
Reshaping sea, remaking land.
A primal force, ancient and bold,
A fiery story, ages old.
