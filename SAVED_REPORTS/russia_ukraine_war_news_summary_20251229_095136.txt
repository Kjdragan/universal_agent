Russia-Ukraine War News Summary - December 29, 2025
======================================================

LATEST DEVELOMENTS (Past Week)
-------------------------------

December 29, 2025:
- Russia claims to have seized the village of Dibrova in Donetsk
- Russian forces shelled Sloviansk, killing at least 1 civilian and injuring 5
- Ukraine launched 91 long-range drones at Russian president's state residence in Novgorod region
- Zelenskyy stated foreign troops in Ukraine are necessary for security guarantees

December 28, 2025:
- Trump and Zelenskyy reportedly closing in on peace proposal to end war
- At least 2 people killed in Kyiv and surrounding region from massive Russian drone/m missile attack

December 27, 2025:
- Ukrainian capital Kyiv came under "massive" Russian attack
- Russian forces conducted large-scale combined missile and drone strike on Ukrainian critical infrastructure

December 25-26, 2025:
- Russian Deputy Foreign Minister Sergei Ryabkov reiterated Kremlin won't sign peace agreements "right now"
- ISW analysis: False reports likely shaping Putin's understanding of battlefield situation

December 24, 2025:
- Zelenskyy proposes compromise for Russian-occupied areas of Ukraine in peace talks

KEY INSIGHTS FROM ANALYSTS

- Russian advances not likely to be linear
- Institute for the Study of War: Will likely take Russian forces longer than 1,190 days to seize all four oblasts
- Casualty reports (Dec 23): Russia 7,175 killed; Ukraine 15,954 killed (including 13,145 in territories)
